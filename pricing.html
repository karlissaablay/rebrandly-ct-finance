Frequently asked questions

Is VaultSecure SOC 2 compliant?

Yes. VaultSecure maintains SOC 2 Type II certification. We undergo annual third-party audits and can provide our latest report under NDA upon request.

What compliance frameworks do you support?

Our compliance engine supports FINRA, OCC, NAIC, CFPB, and state-level banking and insurance regulations. Custom regulatory frameworks can be configured on Enterprise plans.

How does integration work?

VaultSecure offers REST APIs, webhooks, and pre-built connectors for major core banking systems (FIS, Jack Henry, Fiserv) and insurance platforms (Guidewire, Duck Creek).

Can we white-label the customer portal?

Yes. The customer portal is fully white-labeled on Business and Enterprise plans. Your customers see your brand, logo, and colors throughout the experience.

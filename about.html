Built by people who were tired of hidden fees

We started VaultSecure because we believed banking and insurance should be simple, transparent, and actually work for customers. Not against them.

Our story

In 2021, our founders were hit with a $35 overdraft fee for a $4 coffee purchase. The bank had processed a larger transaction first, even though the coffee was bought earlier in the day. It was legal. It was by design. And it was the last straw.

We launched VaultSecure with a simple promise: no hidden fees, no gotcha pricing, and no fine print designed to trick you. We make money by providing genuinely useful financial products at fair rates, not by nickel-and-diming customers when they're not looking.

Today, over 500,000 customers trust us with their money. We've saved our customers an estimated $47 million in fees they would have paid at traditional banks. And we're just getting started.

Security & compliance

Your money and data are protected by the same standards used by the world's largest financial institutions.

FDIC Insured

Your deposits are insured up to $250,000 per depositor by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation. Your money is safe, even if something happens to us.

256-bit Encryption

All data in transit and at rest is protected with AES-256 encryption. The same standard used by the U.S. military and top-tier financial institutions worldwide.

SOC 2 Type II

Independently audited and certified annually. Our controls for security, availability, and confidentiality meet the highest industry standards.

24/7 Fraud Monitoring
